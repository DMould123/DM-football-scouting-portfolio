Player Match Reports

Gessime Yassine

Midfielder

Minutes: 90

Player Rating: 7.5

Scout Report

This was my first opportunity to watch Gessime Yassine live following his impressive displays with Morocco U20, where he played a key role in their success. From an early stage, his confidence in 1v1 situations stood out, regularly demanding the ball and looking to engage his direct opponent.

Yassine showed a strong ability to dribble both inside and outside, adjusting his approach depending on the defender’s body shape. His ball control and balance allowed him to change direction quickly, making him difficult to contain in tight areas.

A clear example of his effectiveness in these situations came in the build-up to the penalty he won. Driving directly at his marker, Yassine shifted the ball sharply, committing the defender before drawing the foul inside the penalty area. This action highlighted his bravery in possession and his willingness to take responsibility in decisive moments.
